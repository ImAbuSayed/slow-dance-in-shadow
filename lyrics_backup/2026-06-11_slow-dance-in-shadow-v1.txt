[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the heat of your skin in the dark
A quiet fire burning right from the start
Pulling you close where the moonlight fades
We are a secret the midnight made

[Verse 1]
The dust settles down on the old wooden floor
I leave the world waiting outside of the door
Your eyes are catching the glow of the flame
Every breath that I take is a spark for your name

[Pre-Chorus]
Shadows are falling, we sway out of time
Your heartbeat is moving in rhythm with mine
I lean in closer to taste the desire
We are just melting away in the fire

[Chorus]
Slow dance in shadow, just you and me
Lost in the warmth of this deep chemistry
I trace the lines of your face in the night
Holding you here till the morning brings light

[Post-Chorus]
Slow down, lean in, do not let me go
Slow down, lean in, moving so slow

[Verse 2]
The scent of the smoke in the sweet evening air
My fingers get lost in the waves of your hair
No need for a word when you look at me so
We speak in the silence that only we know

[Bridge]
